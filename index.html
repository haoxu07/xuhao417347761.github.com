Current Research Projects

Underwater acoustic communication system for manned deep submersible JiaoLong. (The First manned subsmersible for China )

Underwater acoustic transmission of images, human voice, data and texts between vehicle under 7000m depth and surface ship was accomplished of this underwater acoustic communication system.

  • Processed the data received from all sea trials, address the communication problems caused by the deep sea acoustic channel. Proposed soft iterated channel estimation with phase locked loop to track the channel response with rapid phase variation in underwater acoustic communication.
  • Developed the more reliable turbo equalization based on the channel estimation for deep sea coherent communication channel.

    • This project is funded by the National 863 High-Tech Foundation, PI: Min Zhu.

    Underwater acoustic network, Signal processing in physical layer communication Electromagnetic waves can’t be used for data transmission in the ocean, so underwater wireless networks are generally formed by acoustically connected ocean-bottom sensors, autonomous underwater vehicles, and a surface station, which provides a link to control center. Shallow water acoustic channel characteristics, such as low available bandwidth, highly varying multipath, and large propagation delays, restrict the efficiency of UWA networks. My work is concentrated on Time reversal and Turbo equalization.

  • Investigated time-reversal multi-channel combining which can retrace incoming multiple paths in large delay spread channels to simplify the underwater acoustic communication equalization. Proposed whitening filters based on AR process to develop colored gaussian noise into white gaussian noise, which solves the noise model misadjustment of equalization based on channel estimation.
  • Investigated turbo principle of exchanging soft information between decoders and equalizers which can be realized by linear filters and Markov Chain Monte Carlo equalizer. Joint Optimization between Time revervsal and Turbo equalization can have a superior performance over adaptive equalization with turbo decoding and a limited complexity for application.
  • Designed the scheme for data link of the data radio with CSMA/CD protocol. Programmed the GUI on computer to diagnose and supervise the whole aquatic network.

    • This project is funded by the National 863 High-Tech Foundation, PI: Min Zhu.

    Robot for Robot Wars Competition. We design a powerful robot to participate in the famous Robocup Competition (just like the famous TV show: Robot Wars). This kind of robot needs to detect the own positions and the opponet on a fixed platform, then the robot push the opponent away from the platform for victory. To meet these needs, we used the circuits of the microcontroller and sensors, the efficient control algorithm to attact the opponent. Besides, our local computer would record the data from the robot in competition from the platform by the wireless RF module.

  • Designed the hardware of sensor circuits like grey sensors (to detect the position on the grey platform), Infrared distance sensors(to detect the opponent), the circuits of microcontroller.
  • Designed the software of processing the signal from the sensors to provide reliable information to the controller. Advanced scheme is realized in the control program for victory in competition. Programmed the software on STM32 microcontroller.

    • This project won the first prize in national Robocup competition.
